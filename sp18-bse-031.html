SPECIAL NOTICE – CSI PACIFIC FACILITIES AT NEXT PHASE OF RETURN TO SPORT AS OF JUNE 29

CSI Pacific resumed operations at its campuses as of June 15, 2020 and moved to our next stage in concert with the Province of British Columbia moving into Phase 3 of BC’s Restart Plan. As part of our latest plan, masks will now be required indoors at all our CSI Pacific campuses at all times (with the only exceptions being when athletes are exerting themselves). Speaker Series On Demand Now Free to Public

CSI Pacific is happy to announce that they have made their Sport Performance Speaker Series free to the general public. Previously limited to only targeted, high performance athletes and coaches, CSI Pacific has opened the education program up to anyone with an interest in cutting edge sport science, practical solutions to performance barriers, and thoughtful conversations with Canada’s best athletes.

Eight topics and over 70 video & audio files now available!
